About Dino Bus Simulator

Dino Bus Simulator is a 3D bus driving simulation game developed in 2025 by Dino Studio, led by Armindiba. In this game, you will experience driving the popular Volvo B9 bus across beautifully crafted urban and highway routes inspired by Truckers of Europe 3.

🎵 Background Music

The game’s main menu features a peaceful piano piece titled “Silent Journey”, designed to create a calm and immersive atmosphere for players.

🔐 Privacy and User Data

At Dino Bus Simulator, we respect your privacy. No sensitive personal data is collected or shared without your consent. Game-related data such as language preferences, graphics settings, and progress are stored locally on your device only.
